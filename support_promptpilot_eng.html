Frequently asked questions

How does PromptPilot work?

PromptPilot uses Gemini Nano – Google's on-device AI built into Chrome – to improve, structure, and expand prompts directly in your browser. All processing happens on your device, with no external servers involved.

Why does PromptPilot only work in Chrome?

Gemini Nano is a Chrome-exclusive feature and is currently only available in Google Chrome version 127 and above. Other browsers such as Brave, Firefox, or Safari do not support this API.

What are the hardware requirements?

Gemini Nano requires at least 16 GB of RAM and 4 GB of VRAM. When you first activate the extension, Chrome will download the AI model – this may take a few minutes.

Are my texts sent to any servers?

No. All AI processing runs locally via Gemini Nano on your device. Prompt content is never sent to external servers. PromptPilot does not transmit any user data.

Can I use PromptPilot on iPad or mobile?

No. Chrome Extensions are not available on mobile devices, including iPads. PromptPilot is designed exclusively for Chrome on desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux).

Important notes

  • PromptPilot requires Google Chrome – other browsers are not supported.
  • AI-assisted prompt optimization does not replace professional, legal, or privacy review of generated content.
  • Output quality depends on the installed Gemini Nano version and available hardware resources.

Privacy at a glance

  • All AI processing is done locally via Gemini Nano – no cloud, no external servers.
  • There is no tracking, analytics software, or advertising.
  • There is no registration, no user account, and no subscription.
  • Prompt content is not stored or transmitted.

Privacy Policy

This page serves both as the support page and the privacy policy for PromptPilot. The specific browser permissions are additionally described in the Chrome Web Store listing.

What data the extension processes

PromptPilot processes only text that users actively enter into the extension or that is selected in an active tab. Processing is done locally via Chrome's Gemini Nano API.

How this data is processed

Input text is passed to Chrome's local LanguageModel API and processed directly on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers. No content is permanently stored.

Transfer to third parties

No user data or prompt content is transmitted to the developer's own servers or to any third parties. PromptPilot does not establish connections to any external services.

Website content and active tabs

On explicit user action, the extension can import selected text from a webpage. There is no automatic access to browsing history, no background analysis of websites, and no user profiling.

Support requests

If you send a support request by email or through the contact form, the information you provide is used solely to handle your request.

Cookies, tracking, and advertising

The extension itself uses no tracking, no analytics software, and no advertising. This support page uses no cookies and no user accounts.
